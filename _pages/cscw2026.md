This is a companion page for our upcoming paper titled **"Studying Mobile Spatial Collaboration across Video Calls and Augmented Reality"**. This paper has been _**conditionally accepted**_ for publication in the [Proceedings of the ACM on Human Computer Interaction](https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmhci), and will be presented at [CSCW 2026](https://cscw.acm.org/2026/). This research was conducted by the [ACME Lab](https://www.colorado.edu/atlas/acme-lab) in collaboration with, and with funding from, Ericsson Research.
